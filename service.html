Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

How do I get started with a project or request a quote?
Contact us via phone, email, or form. We offer consultation, provide a detailed quote, then schedule and start work.
Do you offer any warranties or guarantees on your work?
Yes, we offer warranties on workmanship and materials used, ensuring quality and durability across construction, renovation, painting, and granite services.
What materials do you use, and can I choose them?
We use high-quality materials for all services, and yes—you can choose preferred brands, finishes, or granite types during consultation.
Can I make changes to the project during construction?
Yes, you can request changes during construction. We'll review feasibility, adjust the quote or timeline if needed, and proceed accordingly.
How do you ensure quality and safety on the job site?
We follow strict safety protocols, use certified materials, employ skilled workers, and conduct regular inspections to ensure top-quality, safe work.
Do you provide free estimates or consultations?
Yes, we provide free estimates and consultations to discuss your project needs, budget, and design preferences before starting work.
Can you help with permits and approvals?
Yes, we assist with obtaining necessary permits and approvals to ensure your project complies with local building codes and regulations.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, we are fully licensed and insured, ensuring your project is protected and meets all legal and safety requirements.
How long does a typical construction project take?
Project timelines vary by scope, but typical construction or renovation projects usually take its best time to complete.
What types of construction services do you offer?
We offer residential and commercial construction, renovations, remodeling, painting, granite installation and flooring services.
